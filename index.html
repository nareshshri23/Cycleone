Our Journey

Milestones that fueled our growth and innovation at CycleOne.

01

IIC SLIET Funding

Founded in Dec 2022 by Raj Tagore & Abhishek Yadav, CycleOne received ₹50K in two phases from IIC SLIET to develop the prototype.

02

SAA Support

Received ₹75K from the Student Alumni Association, enhancing docking stations, app functionality, and security systems.

03

NHPC CSR Grant

NHPC sanctioned ₹11.5 lakhs under CSR, enabling full-scale deployment with smart docks and upgraded cycles.

04

Current Status

Implementation underway: docking stations are being set up. Plans ahead include wider expansion, better tracking, and sustainable growth.
