About Our Project

We are a team of undergraduate students at SLIET dedicated to developing an efficient campus commute system. Our project focuses on the installation of cycle stands across the campus, ensuring the easy availability of bicycles. This initiative aims to promote eco-friendly transportation, reduce dependence on motorized vehicles, and foster a culture of fitness and environmental consciousness within our academic community.
